Owner Bio

Owner- Michael Dicus
I once was thrown onto the stage of Radio City Music Hall and performed for thousands of people with no rehearsal… I once made it to the celebrity judging round for America's Got Talent and failed the competition!
I am Michael Dicus owner of Eec! Productions, Inc, and my point is, I know how to stand in the face of great opportunities in business and tackle all the nerve-wracking obstacles that come with creating success, short or long term.
I also love using entertainment and creativity to solve some of our biggest problems.
Here's another thing that happened in my life, I once cheered for an opposing team at Wembley Arena in London, UK. during a show and got booed horrifically, all in good fun.
Final point, some things just don't work out, and I have a knack to look into the belly of the beast and find a way out.
